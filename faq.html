Technical FAQ

What industries do you serve?

We focus on high-complexity engineering environments: AI/ML development, Robotics, Computer Vision, Aerospace, Defense, and Advanced Manufacturing.

How do you vet technical candidates?

We don't just "match keywords." We conduct deep-dive technical interviews to verify project contributions, problem-solving methodologies, and cultural alignment. We understand the difference between a "good" engineer and a "transformative" one.

What is your fee structure?

Every search is contingency-based. You only pay when we successfully deliver the talent you need. Our placements come with a 90-day performance guarantee.

Do you handle temp or contract roles?

No. We specialize exclusively in permanent, direct-hire placements for leadership and critical engineering roles.
