Frequently Asked Questions

How is Streamline different from other recruiting firms?

We don't do "keyword matching" or high-volume temp fills. We specialize strictly in permanent placements for high-technical roles in AI, Robotics, and Advanced Manufacturing. We vet for technical depth and cultural fit before a candidate ever reaches your inbox.

What is your fee structure?

We operate on a contingency basis. This means our interests are perfectly aligned with yours: you only pay a fee if we successfully place a candidate that you hire. There are no upfront costs to start a search with us.

Which industries do you serve?

Our core expertise lies in AI/Machine Learning, Robotics, Computer Vision, Aerospace, Medical Device Manufacturing, Fintech, and Semiconductor industries. We focus on the intersection of hardware and software.

How long does a typical search take?

While every search is unique, we typically present high-quality, fully-vetted candidates within the first 1-2 weeks. Because we maintain a deep network of passive talent in these specific niches, we move much faster than generalist firms.
